Driving Directions via I-5 N

via exit 168B for WA-520

  1. Take exit 168B for WA-520 towards Bellevue/Kirkland.
  2. Follow WA-520 and take exit toward Montlake Blvd//Univ Of Wash.
  3. Use left two lanes and turn left onto Montlake Blvd E.
  4. Follow Montlake Blvd E and turn slightly left onto 25th Ave NE.
  5. Follow 25th Ave NE and turn left onto Pend Oreille Road NE.
  6. Follow Pend Oreille Road NE and pay for parking.
  7. Follow Pend Oreille Road NE and turn left onto Mason Rd.
  8. Follow Mason Rd and park in the Padelford Parking Garage.

via exit 169 onto NE 45th St

  1. Take exit 169 onto NE 45th St.
  2. Follow signs for NE 45th St and turn right onto NE 45th St.
  3. Follow NE 45th St and turn right onto Memorial Way NE.
  4. Follow Memorial Way NE and pay for parking.
  5. Turn left onto E Stevens Way NE.
  6. Follow E Stevens Way NE and turn left onto Pend Orelle Road NE.
  7. Follow Pend Oreille Road NE and turn right onto Mason Rd.
  8. Follow Mason Rd and park in the Padelford Parking Garage.

Parking

There are several on-campus parking options located near the HUB. We recommend the Padelford Parking Garage if there is space. Otherwise, the E18 lot is more affordable and available. Street parking off-campus can be hard to come by and is usually time-constrained.
